What You Need to Start planting

✔️ Soil (or pots with potting mix)

✔️Seeds or small plants (called seedlings)

✔️Sunlight (at least 4–6 hours a day for most plants)

✔️ Water (regular watering is essential)

✔️ Basic tools (trowel, gloves, watering can)
