What is Gardening?

Gardening is the practice of growing and caring for plants, usually in a yard, balcony, or garden space. It can involve growing flowers, vegetables, herbs, or decorative plants.

What You Need to Start planting

Planting is not only a rewarding hobby, but also a powerful way to connect with nature and improve our environment. Whether you're growing flowers, herbs, or vegetables, taking care of plants teaches patience, responsibility, and brings beauty and life to any space. By starting small and learning as you go, anyone can enjoy the benefits of planting.
